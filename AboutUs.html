Keeping Everyone Safe

To protect people from being harmed or stalked, there will be a friend system that makes it so only the people you allow will be able to see your location. If an app user is not friends with you, your identity will be anonymous. You will have to accept a friend request if you want others to see what event you are going to. Events are also only put on by establishments we have manually approved. Click here to learn more about establishments.
